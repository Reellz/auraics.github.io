About Our School

Aura International Christian School is a premier educational institution committed to academic excellence and spiritual growth. Founded on Christian principles, we provide a nurturing environment where students can thrive intellectually, socially, and spiritually.

Our dedicated faculty and staff are committed to helping each student reach their full potential while developing strong moral character and a lifelong love for learning.

Our Academic Programs

Early Childhood

Our preschool program nurtures young minds through play-based learning and foundational Christian values.

Elementary School

A comprehensive curriculum that builds strong academic foundations and character development.

High School

College-preparatory program with advanced courses and spiritual guidance for young adults.

Our Core Values

Faith

We integrate Christian principles into all aspects of learning and school life.

Excellence

We strive for academic rigor and personal growth in all our programs.

Community

We foster a supportive environment where everyone belongs and thrives.
