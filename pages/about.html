The Challenge We Address

In today's globalized society, many families struggle to find an educational institution that aligns with their values while providing rigorous academic programs. Public and conventional private schools often fail to incorporate spiritual growth and moral education, resulting in students who may excel academically but lack a strong ethical foundation.

Our Christ-Centered Solution

Aura International Christian School offers a curriculum that integrates academic rigor with spiritual and moral education. By employing qualified educators who are committed to both academic excellence and Christian values, we create a nurturing environment where students thrive intellectually and spiritually. Our innovative programs and diverse extracurricular activities, conducted in a homely environment, ensure our students become well-rounded individuals.

About Our ACE Curriculum

AICS uses the Accelerated Christian Education (ACE) curriculum. At AICS, we provide a Christ-centered education using ACE which is an individualized, self-paced, and bible-based programme.

The ACE curriculum helps students develop critical thinking skills while building a strong biblical worldview. Our approach allows each student to progress at their own pace while mastering each concept before moving forward.
