About Aura International C.S

Aura International C.S isn't just a school—it's a home where faith and excellence meet.

Who We Are

The Aura Experience is a bold faith venture that transcends local boundaries. Our identity as Glabal Citizens is anchored in the biblical mandate of Matthew 5:16: Let your light so shine before men, that they may see our good works and glorify your father in Heaven."

We believe that when a student is equipped with the innovative problem-solving skills of the ACE curriculum and the profound depth discipleship, their life becomes a beacon. AICS is not just a school; it is a center of transformation reaching the nations.

Our Story

AICS stems from the Home School of the Bwambales, employing the Accelerated Christian Education Curriculum, which started in 2019 to cater for the unique learning needs of one of their children, who was struggling in the national system. The immediate change in this learner's academic and creative abilities motivated the rest of our children to join the Hill City Home School. After several years of experience and resource building with A.C.E., we sensed God calling us to share these blessings with the rest of His people. The strategic differentiator of AICS is our refusal to separate academic rigor from spiritual formation. In a competitive global economy, our dual focus ensures that our graduates are not only technically proficient but also morally unshakeable. Currently, some of our students are able to instruct others in Music, and one of them built this website.
